Seminars

Weekly Seminar

Yonsei Cosmo-Chalk

  • Time: Every Friday, 2:00 PM
  • Location: 557, Science Hall
  • Website: Yonsei Cosmo-Chalk Github
  • Description: Informal weekly blackboard seminar for Yonsei HEP-COSMO group members. This casual gathering encourages students to present their research, discuss intriguing journal articles, and engage in relaxed discussions about various topics. The seminar fosters a supportive environment for intellectual growth and collaboration among group members.


Indico

Indico for Yonsei-HEP-COSMO

  • Description: Indico is an open-source tool for event organization, archival, and collaboration. It is used by our research group to manage and share information about various events such as conferences, workshops, and seminars. The Indico platform allows us to efficiently organize and disseminate event details, manage registration, and provide a centralized repository for event-related materials. By using Indico, we aim to streamline our event planning process and enhance collaboration within our research community.
